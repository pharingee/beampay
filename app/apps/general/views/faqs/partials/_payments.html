Payments

Which cards are supported?
  • Stripe: all debit and credit cards of Visa, MasterCard, American Express, JCB, Discover, and Diners Club (more info)
  • Paypal: all credit cards of Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, JCB, Diner's Club and EnRoute and all debit cards of Visa and MasterCard (more info)
In which currency will I be charged?
You will be always charged in USD.
Are there any extra charges?
Note that you make a payment to a U.S.-based company in USD.

If you are from another country, your bank might incur some extra charges:
  • for conversion to USD in case your bank uses another currency
  • for making an international payment if you bank is not a U.S. bank
You can find out these charges from your bank (more info).
