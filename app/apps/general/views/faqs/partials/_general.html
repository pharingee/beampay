What can I use Beam for?
Beam helps Ghanaians abroad care for loved ones back home with gifts and bill payments. The way we make a difference is to ensure the money is attributed directly to specific services, like paying for your niece’s school fees, your grandma's electricity bill or topping up your sister's phone credit. In addition, we can even arrange for gift services like buying and delivering a birthday cake for your best friend’s birthday back in Ghana.

Do you need a helping hand on the ground? Beam does all sorts of errands in Ghana on your behalf. Get us to do deliveries, check on your construction project or let us help you with finding service providers or local products.
How does Beam work?
You can place your order with Beam in two ways: You either sign up to Beam via www.beampay.co and pick one of the services. Alternatively, you can reach us directly via WhatsApp (024 145 1551) and just tell us what you need.

A member of the Beam service team will get back to you shortly after you placed your request and iron out all the details and payment.
Can I send money through Beam?
No, Beam never pays out money to individuals.
How can I make a payment on Beam?
You can pay on Beam with any international debit or credit card.
What service fees can I expect on Beam?
Our service charges vary depending on the specific service provided. We always inform you about all the costs, which you have a chance to accept or reject before we send the invoice, so there will be no unpleasant surprises.
In which currency will I be charged?
You will be always charged in USD.
How can I be sure that my payment has been made?
You will receive a receipt for every transaction with Beam, and if you have a specific confirmation method you prefer, we’re happy to provide that as well.
Are there any extra charges?
There might be extra charges if you make payments from outside the U.S. This is because banks outside the U.S. charge customers a small fee for making payments to U.S.-based companies.

If you are from another country, your bank might incur some extra charges:
  • for conversion to USD in case your bank uses another currency
  • for making an international payment if you bank is not a U.S. bank
You can find out these charges from your bank (more info).
Is my personal data safe with Beam?
We protect your personal data by using the latest technology and applying the highest security standards. We use SSL encryption for all communication with our website to make sure that no third party can intercept your data. We never store sensitive credit card data ourselves, but use top-notch third party service providers that are PCI- compliant (i.e. the payment providers that meet the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) and fulfill all regulatory requirements. Your personal data is stored safely on the servers of industry-leading data storage providers.
