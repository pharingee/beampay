ABOUT BEAM PAY

Beam Pay is fintech startup which runs from the Meltwater Incubator in East Legon - Accra, Ghana.

Beam is allowing Ghanaians in the diaspora to pay for bills, services delivery and buy gifts for friends and family back home.

PEOPLE BEHIND BEAM

We're team made up of people who love what we do. The core focus of Beam is to deliver top-notch service to all our clients - home and abroad.

Everyday, we work to ensure that you too can send love back home - wherever you are in the world!
