How does Beam work?

STEP 1: Choose a service

Select a service you want to pay for on behalf of a loved one in Ghana. You can pay for the following services with Beam:

Hospital bills at all major public and private hospitals

School fees at public and private schools, and universities

Small gifts such as cakes, flowers, and goodie hampers

Water and electricity bills

Airtime phone top-ups

Internet access and cable TV

STEP 2: Pay for the service

Pay in seconds with your debit or credit card. Beam will add a small service fee.

IMPORTANT NOTE: This money will NOT be received by the recipient. Rather, Beam will make the payment on your behalf directly to the service provider.

STEP 3: Beam processes payment

Once payment has been made, Beam will ensure that the service provider receives the money, even if it means making a manual payment on your behalf.

Beam pledges to process all payments within 1 business day.

STEP 4: Confirmation and proof of payment

Beam sends proof of payment to both the sender and the recipient in the form of a scanned receipt, a follow-up phone call to both sender and recipient, as well as whatever additional proof the sender requires.

If the payment could not be made for any reason, Beam pledges to return the full amount to the sender.
